<h2 id="exchange-locations">3. Exchange Locations & Method (Crucial! ‚≠ê)</h2>

<h2 id="usage-lines">4. Usage & Available Lines</h2>

<ul>
  <li><b>How to Use</b>: Insert the physical ticket into the ticket gate's slot, pass through, and take the ticket from the other side.</li>
  <li><b>Available Lines</b>: <b>All lines of Tokyo Metro + Toei Subway (13 lines total)</b></li>
</ul>
