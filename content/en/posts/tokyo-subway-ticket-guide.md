## 4. Usage & Available Lines

*   **How to Use**: Simply insert your physical ticket into the ticket gate's slot, pass through, and don't forget to take the ticket as it pops out from the other side. You can use it as many times as you want within the validity period.
*   **Available Lines**: **All lines of Tokyo Metro + Toei Subway (13 lines in total)**
