## STEP 3. Getting to Central Tokyo

<p>With your transport card ready, it's time to head to your hotel. Choose the best option based on your travel style and luggage.</p>

<p><strong>Option 1: Keikyu Line (Turn right from arrivals)</strong></p>
<p>Take the Shinagawa-bound train. Transfer to the JR Yamanote Line for easy access to Shinjuku, Shibuya, Tokyo Station, and more. All train types (Local, Express, Limited Express, Rapid Express, Airport Express) stop at Shinagawa.</p>

<p><strong>Option 2: Tokyo Monorail (Turn left from arrivals)</strong></p>
<p>Take the Hamamatsucho-bound monorail. It costs more (520 yen) but offers ocean views along the route. Free for JR Pass holders.</p>
