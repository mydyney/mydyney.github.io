## 6. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

<strong>Q. What's the shelf life of baumkuchen?</strong><br>
A: Baumkuchen can be stored at room temperature with a shelf life of about 2 weeks, making it perfect for souvenirs back home.

<strong>Q. Can I buy individual pieces?</strong><br>
A: Yes! The cutting baumkuchen (360 yen) contains 4 pieces, perfect for tasting or sharing with coworkers.

<strong>Q. Can I bring it on a plane?</strong><br>
A: Baumkuchen is allowed in carry-on luggage. However, pudding is classified as liquid/gel and absolutely cannot be carried on - you must check it or consume it locally.

<strong>Q. Can I get tax-free (Tax Free)?</strong><br>
A: Unfortunately, Jiichiro KITTE location is not a tax-free eligible store (according to KITTE official information). Tax refunds are not available.
