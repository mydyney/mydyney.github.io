## 3. Where to Board the Limousine Bus at Narita

<p>So you don't get lost in the airport, here's a detailed guide to boarding platforms by terminal and the step-by-step process.</p>

<ol>
  <li><strong>Head to the Arrivals Lobby:</strong> After collecting your luggage and clearing immigration, exit to the 1st floor arrivals lobby of your terminal.</li>
  <li><strong>Purchase Ticket:</strong> If you have an online booking QR code, head straight to the boarding area! For on-site purchases, buy your ticket at the 1st floor counter or automated machine.</li>
  <li><strong>Go to Your Platform:</strong> Check the platform number on your ticket, then walk outside and wait at the designated platform.</li>
  <li><strong>Check Luggage & Board:</strong> At the platform, staff will tag your suitcase with a destination label and load it into the bus trunk. Make sure to keep the baggage claim ticket!</li>
</ol>

## Wrap-Up: Frequently Asked Questions

<p><strong>Q1. Is traffic a big problem with the limousine bus?</strong></p>
<p>A. Rush-hour traffic can cause delays, so give yourself extra time ‚Äî especially when heading back to the airport.</p>

<p><strong>Q2. How many suitcases can I bring?</strong></p>
<p>A. Generally one suitcase per person is allowed. Oversized or extra bags may incur an additional fee, so check with the operator.</p>

<p><strong>Q3. Do I need to exchange my online booking for a physical ticket?</strong></p>
<p>A. QR codes from platforms like Klook can typically be used to board directly ‚Äî no exchange required, which makes things very convenient.</p>
