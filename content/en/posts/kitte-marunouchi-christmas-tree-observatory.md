## How to Get to KITTE

<p>KITTE is connected to Tokyo Station's Marunouchi Line via an underground passage, making the Marunouchi Line the most convenient option. Head toward the Marunouchi South Exit and you'll see KITTE right away.</p>

<p>It's also within a 10-minute walk from JR Tokyo Station, Nijubashimae Station, Otemachi Station, and Yurakucho Station.</p>
