<h2>ğŸŒ¿ Morning Course: Urban Oasis, Shinjuku Gyoen</h2>

Located in the middle of Shinjuku's building forest, **Shinjuku Gyoen** features British, French, and Japanese style gardens, making it the perfect place to escape the busy city for a while.

- Admission: Adult 500 yen
- Closed: Every Monday (If Monday is a holiday, closed the following weekday)
- Hours: 9:00 AM ~ 4:00 PM (Varies by season)

**ğŸ“Œ Tip:** Bring a picnic mat to relax on the grass, or enjoy coffee with a park view at **Starbucks Shinjuku Gyoen**.

<h3>ğŸ›ï¸ Shopping Spots (Department Stores/Specialty Shops)</h3>

Shinjuku has large department stores with different charms at each exit.

- **East Exit:** Focus on luxury and trends. **Isetan** (Luxury), **Lumine EST** (Young fashion).
- **South Exit:** Good for families. **Takashimaya** (Classic), **NEWoMan** (Stylish/Curated), **Lumine 1 & 2**.
- **West Exit:** **Keio** and **Odakyu** department stores (Directly connected to station, famous for food/souvenirs).

- **Golden Gai:** Over 200 tiny bars tightly packed. A unique culture where you pay a cover charge to drink in a 5-seat bar.
