## #3. Shinjuku Gyoen â€” A Cherry Blossom Garden in the Heart of the City

## #6. Roppongi Sakurazaka & Tokyo Midtown Cherry Blossoms

<p>Roppongi has two cherry blossom spots.</p>

<p><strong>Sakurazaka</strong> is a cherry blossom-lined hill behind Roppongi Hills. As the name suggests (sakura = cherry blossom), the road was designed for cherry trees, with blossoms densely lining both sides. Barely any tourists visit, making it a peaceful spot for a quiet walk.</p>

<p><strong>Tokyo Midtown</strong> features about a <strong>200-meter cherry blossom path</strong> alongside the Midtown Garden. Every cherry blossom season, they host a <strong>large-scale illumination event</strong>. The pink-lit cherry blossom path is famous as a night viewing spot.</p>

<ul>
  <li>Best plan: Sakurazaka during the day, Midtown for night cherry blossoms</li>
  <li>Both within a 5-minute walk from Roppongi Station</li>
