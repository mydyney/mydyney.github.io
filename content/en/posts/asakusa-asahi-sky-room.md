## ğŸ« Asahi Sky Room Waiting System Guide

<p><strong>[Step 1] Get Your Number Ticket at the 22F Sky Room Entrance</strong></p>

<p>Asahi Sky Room operates on a number ticket system. You can scan the QR code to check real-time waiting status, so you can explore the area while waiting.</p>

<ul>
  <li>Only 1 ticket per group</li>
  <li>Maximum 4 people per ticket</li>
  <li>Tickets are valid only on the day of issue and cannot be reissued if lost</li>
</ul>
