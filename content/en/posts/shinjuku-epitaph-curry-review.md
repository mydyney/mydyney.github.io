## 1. Golden Gai Legend, Moved to Shinjuku-Sanchome!

'Epitaph Curry' was originally a legendary late-night restaurant that only opened at night in Shinjuku's Golden Gai.

However, in May 2020, it officially moved near Shinjuku-sanchome Station (2nd Sigma Building B1F) and now opens during the day too!

<strong>Pork Vindaloo (ãƒãƒ¼ã‚¯ãƒ“ãƒ³ãƒ€ãƒ«ãƒ¼)</strong> is not Japanese roux curry but a <strong>spice curry</strong> with intense spices. <strong>It's a flavor explosion of strong sourness, umami, and spiciness.</strong>

<strong>Keema</strong> means <strong>minced meat</strong> in Hindi or Urdu.

<strong>Keema curry</strong>, unlike the chunk meat curry we know, is minced meat (usually pork or beef) stir-fried with onions, tomatoes, and spices into a <strong>dry curry without broth</strong>.

The sauce isn't saucy, and the minced meat is seasoned moderately, making it perfect for mixing over rice.
