## 1. Tokyo Whisky Shopping: Why Shinjuku?

If you're planning whisky shopping in Tokyo, Shinjuku is almost a must-visit destination.

Multiple liquor shops are within walking distance from JR Shinjuku Station, making it possible to do a **"liquor shop pilgrimage"**.

Shinjuku liquor shops especially stock limited whiskies and Japanese whiskies like Yamazaki and Hibiki that are hard to find at airport duty-free shops.
