<p style="text-align: center; font-size: 1.1rem; color: #555;">Azabudai Hills is one of Tokyo's newest landmarks, packed with an observatory, shopping, restaurants, hotels, and galleries all in one place. If you love bread, Comme'N Tokyo in the Azabudai Hills Market is an absolute must-visit.</p>

<p>Comme'N Tokyo was selected for the Tabelog Bread Top 100 in 2022 (Setagaya flagship location). It's run by Chef Shuichi Osawa, the overall winner of the 2019 Mondial du Pain, one of the world's most prestigious baking competitions. The bakery puts out over 100 varieties of bread every day and has become a mandatory stop on any Tokyo bakery tour.</p>
