<p>The <strong>Haneda Airport AMEX Centurion Lounge has changed its opening time from 8AM to 5AM</strong>. This significantly improves accessibility for early morning flight passengers and long-haul transit travelers. Here's a breakdown of the updated hours and time-based usage strategies.</p>

<hr>

<h2>AMEX Lounge Hours Update (5AM-10PM)</h2>

<p>The Haneda Airport AMEX Centurion Lounge was previously known to operate from 08:00-22:00, but as of November 2025, the official digital signage on-site displays:</p>

<blockquote>
<strong>OPEN DAILY 5AM–10PM</strong>
</blockquote>

  <img src="/images/posts/haneda-amex-centurion-lounge-hours-2025-02.jpg" alt="Haneda Airport AMEX Centurion Lounge official hours 5AM-10PM digital signage">
  <figcaption style="font-size: 0.7em; text-align: center;">Official digital signage showing 5AM-10PM operating hours</figcaption>
</figure>

<hr>

<h2>Location (Near Gate 114)</h2>

<p><strong>Location Details:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>Haneda Airport Terminal 3 (International)</li>
  <li>Airside, 3rd Floor (after immigration)</li>
  <li><strong>Near Gate 114 → Take escalator to 4th Floor → The Centurion Lounge</strong></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Quick Directions:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>Complete security screening and immigration</li>
  <li><strong>Head straight toward Gate 114</strong> in the duty-free area</li>
  <li>Follow "LOUNGE" signs to <strong>4th floor</strong></li>
  <li>Look for the <strong>blue Centurion sign</strong> next to JAL Sakura Lounge</li>
</ul>

<h2>Time-Based Usage Strategies</h2>

<h3>1) 5:00AM-8:00AM — Best for Early Morning Flights</h3>
<ul>

  <li>Fewer crowds, easy to secure seating</li>
  <li>Previously only TIAT Lounge was available; now <strong>AMEX Lounge is an early morning option</strong></li>
