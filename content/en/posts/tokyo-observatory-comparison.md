## <strong>2Ô∏è‚É£ Walk Above the Clouds: Tokyo Skytree (TOKYO SKYTREE)</strong>

<strong style="color: #004e82;">"Japan's tallest at 634m‚Äîoverwhelming scale"</strong>

Tokyo Skytree is Japan's tallest freestanding broadcast tower. On clear days, you can see not only Tokyo city but also Mt. Fuji in the distance. Beyond the observatory, the lower-level shopping mall Solamachi and aquarium are connected, making it perfect for combining sightseeing, shopping, and dining.

<ul>
  <li><strong>Recommended for:</strong> Those wanting to experience "the highest point," families with children, shopping enthusiasts</li>
  <li><strong>Features:</strong> At 634m, it's Japan's tallest. On clear days, you can see Mt. Fuji, and it's perfect for a half-day course.</li>
</ul>
