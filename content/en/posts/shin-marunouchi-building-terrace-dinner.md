## How to Get to HOUSE

HOUSE is on the 7th floor of the Shin-Marunouchi Building. The building is directly connected underground to the north exit of Tokyo Station on the Marunouchi Line. JR Tokyo Station, Otemachi Station, and Nijubashimae Station are all within a 10-minute walk.

If you've visited KITTE or the Marunouchi Building, the Shin-Marunouchi Building is the one across the street with the BEAMS store.

HOUSE is open until 11:00 PM, but the Tokyo Station illumination only runs from sunset until 9:00 PM. Keep this in mind when planning your visit.
