## 4Ô∏è‚É£ Viewing Day Checklist (Q&A)

<strong>Q. Until when do trains run on December 31st?</strong>

A. Major Tokyo trains (JR, Tokyo Metro, etc.) operate <strong>all-night service</strong> from the night of December 31st through the morning of January 1st. No worry about not being able to get home, but intervals are longer than usual, so travel with time to spare.

<strong>Q. Is it very cold?</strong>

A. Yes, it's cold! The area around the government building has strong wind (building wind) blowing between high-rise buildings. <strong>Long padding, hot packs, gloves</strong> are essential. You'll be standing outdoors for over 2 hours.

<strong>Q. What if I lose the lottery?</strong>

A. Don't be too disappointed! While you can't enter the main plaza, you can still fully feel the festival atmosphere on the <strong>Shinjuku Neon Walk</strong> streets around the government building.
