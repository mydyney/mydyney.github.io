<h2>âœ¨ Adults' Stylish Hideout, Yebisu (Charm & Access)</h2>

<p>Unlike the energetic vibe of Shibuya, Yebisu possesses a <strong>calm and sophisticated charm</strong>. As the birthplace of Japan's premium beer "Yebisu," it serves as an "adults' hideout" lined with high-end restaurants and bars, centered around the <strong>red brick buildings</strong> and European-style plaza of "Yebisu Garden Place."</p>

<ul>
<li><p><strong>Access</strong>: Exit from <strong>Yebisu Station's East Exit (JR Yamanote Line or Tokyo Metro Hibiya Line)</strong> and take the "Yebisu Skywalk" to reach Garden Place comfortably in about 5 minutes.</p></li>
</ul>

<p>An <strong>indoor alley-style izakaya street</strong> located near the East Exit of Yebisu Station.
