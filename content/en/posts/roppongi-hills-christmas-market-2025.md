<h2>Roppongi Hills Christmas Market 2025: Essential Information</h2>

<p>Here's the official schedule for the 2025 Roppongi Hills Christmas Market. This authentic German market celebrates its 19th year.</p>

<div class="info-box">
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Dates:</strong> November 22 (Sat) – December 25 (Thu), 2025</li>
    <li><strong>Hours:</strong> 11:00 – 21:00 daily</li>
    <li><strong>Location:</strong> Roppongi Hills O-yane Plaza</li>
    <li><strong>Admission:</strong> Free (food, drinks, and goods sold separately)</li>
    <li><strong>Scale:</strong> 10 booths total (4 shopping, 6 food)</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<h2>Location & How to Get There</h2>

<p>The Christmas Market is held at <strong>Roppongi Hills O-Yane Plaza</strong>, located near the Mori Building entrance—easy to find!</p>

<p><strong>Tokyo Metro Hibiya Line (H) Roppongi Station:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>Use Exit 1C for direct indoor connection to Roppongi Hills</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Toei Oedo Line (E) Roppongi Station:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>Exit 3, then walk 270m along the street</li>
</ul>
