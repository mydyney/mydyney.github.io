## ‚ú® Nakameguro: Charm & Access

<p style="text-align: center; font-size: 1.05rem; color: #333; line-height: 1.8;">
Nakameguro is a <strong>representative emotional neighborhood of Tokyo</strong><br>
Where you can escape the hustle and bustle of Shibuya or Shinjuku<br>
And enjoy leisurely walks, shopping, and gourmet food<br>
Along the serene Meguro River.<br>
<br>
The landscape created by small, unique shops lining the riverbank<br>
And cherry blossom trees is healing in itself.</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>üöÉ Access:</strong> It starts right after getting off at <strong>Nakameguro Station</strong> on the <strong>Tokyu Toyoko Line</strong> or <strong>Tokyo Metro Hibiya Line</strong>.</li>
  <li><strong>‚ú® Charm Points:</strong> Stylish select shops, cute stationery stores, cafes with delicious coffee, and atmospheric restaurants are gathered along the river, making it fun just to walk around.</li>
</ul>

## üö∂‚Äç‚ôÄÔ∏è Half-Day Complete! Recommended Nakameguro Walking Course

<p>I propose the most efficient walking course so even those with no sense of direction won't get lost. Just follow this order to experience the core of Nakameguro.</p>

<ol>
  <li>Start at <strong>Nakameguro Station</strong> ‚Üí Move towards Meguro River</li>
  <li>Walk along the river towards <strong>Starbucks Reserve Roastery</strong> (About 15 minutes while sightseeing)</li>
  <li>After coffee time at Starbucks, return via the opposite riverbank path and fully explore <strong>Shops & Select Shops</strong></li>
  <li>When you get hungry, have lunch or dinner at a <strong>restaurant</strong> along the river</li>
  <li>If you have time, explore <strong>Daikanyama</strong> which is a 15-minute walk away</li>
</ol>

## ÔøΩ Waiting for Spring, Nakameguro Cherry Blossom Season Tips

<p>Nakameguro is famous as the best cherry blossom spot in Tokyo. Every year from late March to early April, the cherry blossoms forming a tunnel on both sides of the Meguro River are truly spectacular.</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Season:</strong> Usually last week of March ~ First week of April</li>
  <li><strong>Caution:</strong> During the cherry blossom season, it is crowded without room to step, so if you want a leisurely trip, avoiding this period is also a way. Rather, if you visit in other seasons, you can feel the quiet charm of Nakameguro more deeply.</li>
</ul>

## üí° 'Daikanyama' Good to Bundle Together

<p>If you walk about 15 minutes along the hill from Nakameguro, you can reach <strong>'Daikanyama'</strong>, a neighborhood with another charm. If Nakameguro has a cute riverbank sensibility, Daikanyama is characterized by luxury boutiques, sophisticated select shops, and complex cultural spaces represented by 'Tsutaya Books'. We recommend a course of spending half a day in Nakameguro and half a day in Daikanyama.</p>
