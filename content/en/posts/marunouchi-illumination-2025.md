<p><strong>Q1. Is there an admission fee?</strong><br>
A. No, illumination viewing, Disney monument viewing, and stamp rally participation are all free. However, purchasing food/drinks at the Christmas Market incurs costs.</p>

<p><strong>Q2. Does it operate in rain?</strong><br>
A. Yes, the illumination operates even in rain. However, the Christmas Market may change depending on weather conditions.</p>

<p><strong>Q3. Can I take photos?</strong><br>
A. Yes, photography is freely allowed. However, tripod use may be restricted.</p>

<p><strong>Q4. What are the gifts for completing the Disney Stamp Rally?</strong><br>
A. 2025 limited edition Disney original goods are provided. Limited to first 2,000 people, so hurry!</p>
