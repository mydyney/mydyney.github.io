## 2. How to Get There (Complete Transportation Guide)

I highly recommend walking from Shinjuku Station West Exit to Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building! üëë

**üöÖ JR Shinjuku Station West Exit**
- Exit from the West Exit and walk straight along **'Chuo-dori'**.
- It's the perfect course that lets you enjoy neon art from Zone A in order, naturally leading to the Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building observation deck. (About 10-15 minutes on foot)

<figure>
  <img src="/images/posts/shinjuku-neon-walk-tokyo-illumination-2025-2026-04.jpg" alt="Chuo-dori street connecting from Shinjuku Station West Exit">
  <figcaption style="font-size: 0.7em; text-align: center;">Chuo-dori connected to Shinjuku Station West Exit</figcaption>
</figure>

**üöÖ Toei Oedo Line (E) Tochomae Station Exit A1**
- It's the closest station to Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building, but you'd have to walk backwards against the flow to reach the Neon Walk venue, disrupting the viewing experience. I recommend using this when heading home after viewing.

**üöÖ Marunouchi Line (M) Nishi-Shinjuku Station Exit E7:** 10-minute walk

**üöå Bus Option** (Recommended if walking is difficult)
- JR Shinjuku Station West Exit (underground bus terminal) ‚Üí Board Toei Bus 'Metropolitan Government Building Loop' ‚Üí Get off at 'Metropolitan Government Building No. 1'
