## 4. Kiosk Ordering (English Available!)

<p>Once it's your turn and you step inside, you'll see a kiosk (ticket machine) near the entrance. It supports 4 languages ‚Äî including English ‚Äî so ordering is a breeze!</p>

<p>Select your language on the screen, choose your dish, pay by card, and a meal ticket will print out. Hand the ticket to the chef, and you're all set!</p>

## 6. The Secret Behind the Flavor: Premium Ingredients

<p>The deep, complex flavor of Iruka ramen comes from the finest ingredients sourced from across Japan.</p>

<ul>
<li>Chicken broth: Nagoya Cochin, Black Satsuma, and other heritage breeds (3 types)</li>
<li>Beef broth: Matsusaka beef from Mie Prefecture</li>
<li>Shrimp broth: Ise shrimp and white wine</li>
<li>Clam broth: Shijimi clams, asari clams, and Miyajima mussels</li>
<li>Other: Kyoto Kujo green onions, Hinai heritage chicken eggs, Kochi yuzu, and more</li>
<li>Water: Purified with a dedicated "Super Bone Filter" system</li>
</ul>
