<h2>üó∫Ô∏è How to Get to Toranomon Hills TOKYO NODE 45F</h2>

<p>Tokyo Node Gallery is located on the 45th floor of Toranomon Hills Station Tower.</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Subway: Directly connected to Tokyo Metro Hibiya Line (H) 'Toranomon Hills Station'</strong></li>
</ul>

<figure>
  <img src="/images/posts/toranomon-hills-hermes-event-2025-18.jpg" alt="Tokyo Metro Hibiya Line (H) Toranomon Hills Station Connecting Passage">
  <figcaption>Tokyo Metro Hibiya Line (H) Toranomon Hills Station Connecting Passage</figcaption>
</figure>

<p><strong>üìç How to Get to 45F (Important)</strong></p>
<p>If this is your first time, it might be a bit confusing. Here's the easiest route I personally took:</p>

<ol>
  <li>From basement or 1F, take <strong>C elevator or shuttle elevator</strong> to 7F.</li>
  <li>From 7F Sky Lobby, <strong>take escalator to 8F TOKYO NODE entrance</strong>.</li>
  <li>From 8F, transfer to high-rise elevator to go up to 45F.</li>
</ol>
