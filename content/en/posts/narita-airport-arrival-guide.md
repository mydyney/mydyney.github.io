<h2>üõ¨ STEP 1. Withdrawing Cash (Finding ATMs)</h2>

<p>The first thing you should do after passing through arrivals is prepare at least a small amount of cash. Japan still has many shops that only accept cash, and topping up transportation cards is mostly possible only with cash.</p>

<p><strong> ATM Locations:</strong> SEVEN Bank ATMs are available in all terminals. However, please note that AEON Bank does <strong>not</strong> have ATMs in Terminal 2!</p>
