## üö∂‚Äç‚ôÇÔ∏è Odaiba-Ariake-Toyosu Perfect One-Day Itinerary

**1. [Early Morning] Start with Toyosu Onsen (Yurikamome Shijo-mae Station)**

Begin your day at **Toyosu Senkaku Banrai & Manyo Club** morning onsen plan. Bathing while overlooking Tokyo Bay is an unforgettable experience.

**2. [Morning] Breakfast at Toyosu Market (Yurikamome Shijo-mae Station)**

After the onsen, head to **Toyosu Market** next door. Enjoy fresh sushi and kaisen-don at famous spots like Sushi-dai for a hearty breakfast.

**3. [Mid-Morning] teamLab Planets Media Art Experience (Yurikamome Shin-Toyosu Station)**

**4. [Afternoon] Shopping & Rest at Ariake (Yurikamome Ariake Station)**

Take the Yurikamome to **Ariake Garden**, exploring the Kanto region's largest **MUJI flagship store** and enjoying leisurely shopping.

**5. [Late Afternoon] Odaiba Core Attractions (Yurikamome Daiba Station)**

Visit the life-size **Gundam at DiverCity** and take Instagram-worthy photos at the **Statue of Liberty** in the seaside park.

**6. [Evening] Finish with Odaiba Night Views (Get off: Odaiba Kaihinkoen Station)**

End your perfect day enjoying the romantic night view of Rainbow Bridge and Tokyo Tower from the waterfront.

**üóΩ Statue of Liberty: Symbol of Friendship from 'Paris', Not New York**

- **Plot Twist:** Symbolizes friendship with Paris, France - not New York.
- **TMI:** In 1998, temporarily borrowed from Paris for 'Year of France in Japan' celebrations. Became so popular that with France's permission, a permanent replica was erected in 2000.
