<p>Ivorish originally started in Fukuoka but now operates only the Tokyo Shibuya location. As a popular brunch cafe, customers start lining up before opening time.</p>

<h2>üçΩÔ∏è Recommended Ivorish Menu Items</h2>

<h3>Lemon Custard French Toast (Shibuya Exclusive)</h3>

<p>This is the signature menu item available only at the Shibuya location. The "Lemon Custard French Toast" I ordered paired with the "Garden Salad" created a fantastic combination.</p>

<p>The French toast features a fluffy texture perfectly harmonized with lemon custard cream, fresh cream, ice cream, and pistachios. The refreshing lemon preserve adds a bright note, creating a balance of sweetness with a clean finish.</p>

<p>The Garden Salad comes loaded with bacon, providing an excellent sweet-and-savory balance. Eating it together with the French toast prevents the sweetness from becoming overwhelming.</p>
