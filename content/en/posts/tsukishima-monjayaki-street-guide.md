<h2>5. FAQ (Q&A)</h2>

<p><b>Q. What is the difference between Okonomiyaki and Monjayaki?</b></p>

<p>A. Okonomiyaki is fried thickly like a 'pancake', but Monjayaki spreads dough with a lot of moisture thinly and grills it as if boiling it down. It is fun to scrape and eat with a small dedicated spatula (hagashi)!</p>

<p><b>Q. Do I have to grill it myself?</b></p>

<p>A. In most shops, the staff grills it for you. Watching the expert's touch is also great fun, so don't worry.</p>
