### Method 2: On-Site Ticket Machine Purchase

<p>Useful when you missed online booking or need to change plans urgently. Available at ticket machines at major Odakyu Line stations like Shinjuku.</p>

<div style="display: grid; grid-template-columns: 1fr; gap: 10px; margin: 1.5rem 0;">
  <p><strong>1. Touch 'Limited Express Ticket'.</strong></p>
  <p><strong>2. Touch 'Purchase' button.</strong> (Up to 8 people)</p>
  <p><strong>3. Select the desired date.</strong></p>
  <p><strong>4. Select the boarding station.</strong></p>
  <p><strong>5. Confirm date and station, then touch OK.</strong></p>
  <p><strong>6. Select the destination station.</strong></p>
  <p><strong>7. Select the number of tickets.</strong></p>
  <p><strong>8. Select the time slot.</strong></p>
  <p><strong>9. Select the train.</strong></p>
  <p><strong>10. Select your seat.</strong> (Ordinary / Panorama Front / Panorama Rear)</p>
  <p><strong>11. Select desired seats and touch OK.</strong></p>
  <p><strong>12. Confirm details and touch OK.</strong></p>
  <p><strong>13. Pay with credit card, IC card, or cash.</strong></p>
</div>

## 3. Romance Car Booking to Boarding A to Z üöâ

<p>After purchasing your ticket, follow these steps to board.</p>

### Step 1. Prepare Tickets (2 Types Required!)

<p>You MUST have two types of tickets to board.</p>
<p><strong>1. Boarding Pass:</strong> Basic fare (Hakone Free Pass, Suica, etc.)</p>
<p><strong>2. Limited Express Ticket:</strong> Seat reservation (Online or paper ticket)</p>

### Step 2. Pass Through Ticket Gate

<p>Use your <strong>boarding pass (Free Pass or IC card)</strong> at the automatic gate. Do not insert the limited express ticket!</p>
