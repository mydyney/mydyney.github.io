## 1. Why Meguro River Cherry Blossoms Are Tokyo's Best

<p>The Meguro River is lined with <strong>over 800 Somei Yoshino cherry trees</strong> stretching along a roughly 4 km path. The sight of pale pink petals blanketing the river surface is one of the most iconic springtime images in Japan. The daytime scenery is spectacular on its own, but what truly sets this place apart is the <strong>enchanting yozakura (night cherry blossoms)</strong> created by the evening light-up along the riverbanks.</p>

<p><strong>Tokyo Mate Tip:</strong> Nakameguro goes beyond simple flower viewing. The stylish riverside cafes and curated boutiques create a cultural experience that earns it the title of Tokyo's premier cherry blossom destination.</p>
