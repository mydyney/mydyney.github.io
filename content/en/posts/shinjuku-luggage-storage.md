## 2. Shinjuku Station Coin Locker Locations

JR Shinjuku Station has many coin lockers installed. Congestion varies by location, so it's convenient to know locker locations in advance. Here are the most accessible areas.

### 1. JR Shinjuku Station New South Gate - 2 Locations (2F)

- 2 locations near JR Shinjuku Station 2F New South Gate
- Connected to highway bus terminal 'Busta Shinjuku'
- South exit (South Gate) fills up quickly
- Recommended to use lockers near New South Gate

### 2. JR Shinjuku Station East Gate, Central Gate - 4 Locations (B1F)

JR Shinjuku Station B1F has coin lockers at 4 locations near East Gate and Central East Gate (one inside the gate). Convenient when heading to Shinjuku's east exit or Kabukicho area.

### 3. Keio Line Shinjuku Station - 10 Locations (B1F)

Keio Line and Inokashira Line Shinjuku Station, accessible from JR Shinjuku Station B1F, also have coin lockers.

Around Shinjuku Station, there are also several staffed **luggage storage services (storage centers)** where you can safely store your luggage. Great when all station coin lockers are full, or when you want to send luggage to the airport or hotel.

### 1. Odakyu Sightseeing Service Center (West/South Exit)

The famous 'Odakyu Sightseeing Service Center' where you board the Hakone Romance Car also offers luggage storage services.

- Directly connected to the station - best accessibility
- Most convenient for Hakone day trips or overnight stays - store large luggage and travel light
- Dimensions: Total of length + width + height up to 160cm, weight up to 25kg
- Maximum storage: 3 days (additional fee per day if exceeded)
