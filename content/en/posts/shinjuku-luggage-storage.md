## 1. Basic Coin Locker Usage (Key-Type vs Touch Panel)

There are 2 types of coin lockers in Japan.

### 1. Key-Type (Old Model)

- Most common type
- Only accepts 100 yen coins
- Put luggage in ‚Üí Insert coins ‚Üí Turn key to lock
- Don't lose the key!

### 2. Touch Panel Type (New Model)

- Increasingly common type
- Accepts both cash and transportation cards (Suica, Pasmo)
- Put luggage in ‚Üí Select location on screen ‚Üí Pay ‚Üí Lock

## 2. Shinjuku Station Coin Locker Locations

JR Shinjuku Station has many coin lockers installed. Congestion varies by location, so it's convenient to know locker locations in advance. Here are the most accessible lockers.

### 1. JR Shinjuku Station Shin-Minami Gates - 2 Locations (2F)

- 2 locations installed near JR Shinjuku Station 2F Shin-Minami Gates
- Connected to highway bus terminal 'Busta Shinjuku'
- South exit (South Gate) fills up quickly, so
- We recommend using lockers near Shin-Minami Gates

### 2. JR Shinjuku Station East Gate, Central Gate - 4 Locations (B1F)

JR Shinjuku Station B1F has coin lockers at 4 total locations near East Gate and Central-East Gate (one inside the gates). Convenient when heading to Shinjuku East Exit or Kabukicho area.

### 3. Keio Line Shinjuku Station - 10 Locations (B1F)

Keio Line and Inokashira Line Shinjuku Station, accessible from JR Shinjuku Station B1F, also have coin lockers.

You can check locker sizes and vacancy status for all 10 locations on the official website, so no need to wander looking for empty lockers.

## 3. Shinjuku Station Luggage Storage Centers

Around Shinjuku Station, there are several **Shinjuku Station luggage storage services (luggage storage centers)** with staff where you can safely store your luggage. Good to use when all station coin lockers are occupied, or when you want to send luggage to the airport or hotel.

### 1. Odakyu Tourist Service Center (West/South Exit)

The famous 'Odakyu Tourist Service Center' where you can board the Hakone Romance Car also operates luggage storage services.

- Directly connected to the station - best accessibility
- Most convenient for Hakone day trips or 1-night stays - store large luggage and travel light
- Width + Length + Height total 160cm, Weight under 25kg
- Maximum storage period: 3 days (additional charge per day if exceeded)
