## 1. Basic Coin Locker Usage (Key-type vs Touch Panel)

Japanese coin lockers come in 2 types.

### 1. Key-type (Traditional)

- Most common type
- **100 yen coins only**
- Insert luggage ‚Üí Insert coins ‚Üí Turn key to lock
- **Don't lose the key!**

### 2. Touch Panel Type (Modern)

- Increasingly common
- Accepts **cash and IC cards** (Suica, PASMO)
- Insert luggage ‚Üí Select location on screen ‚Üí Pay ‚Üí Lock
- Use the same IC card to retrieve luggage

## 2. Shinjuku Station Coin Locker Locations

JR Shinjuku Station has many coin lockers installed throughout. Since congestion varies by location, it's helpful to know the locker locations in advance. Here are the most accessible areas.

### 1. JR Shinjuku Station New South Gate Area - 2 Locations (2F)

- Located near JR Shinjuku Station 2F New South Gate (2 locations)
- Connected to "Busta Shinjuku" (expressway bus terminal)
- South Exit (South Gate) fills up quickly, so
- **New South Gate lockers are recommended**

### 2. JR Shinjuku Station East Gate & Central East Gate - 4 Locations (B1F)

JR Shinjuku Station B1F has coin lockers at 4 locations near the East Gate and Central East Gate (one inside the gate). Convenient for Shinjuku East Exit or Kabukicho area.

### 3. Keio Line Shinjuku Station - 10 Locations (B1F)

Keio Line and Inokashira Line Shinjuku Station (accessible from JR Shinjuku B1F) also have coin lockers.

With 10 locations total, you can check locker sizes and availability on the official website - no need to wander around!

## 3. Shinjuku Station Luggage Storage Centers

Around Shinjuku Station, there are several **staffed luggage storage services (storage centers)** where you can safely store your luggage. Great when station coin lockers are full, or when you want to ship luggage to airports or hotels.

### 1. Odakyu Tourist Service Center (West/South Exit)

The famous 'Odakyu Tourist Service Center' for Hakone Romancecar also offers luggage storage.

- Directly connected to the station - best accessibility
- Perfect for Hakone day trips or overnight stays - store large luggage and travel light
- Size limit: 160cm (L+W+H), 25kg max weight
- Maximum storage: 3 days (additional fees for overstay)
