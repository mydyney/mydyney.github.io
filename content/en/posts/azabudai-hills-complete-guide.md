## ‚ú® What is Azabudai Hills? (Quick Summary)

Azabudai Hills is not just a shopping mall‚Äîit's a <strong>'massive vertical city'</strong>. Created by Mori Building (the company behind Roppongi Hills) over 30 years, it integrates offices, residences, commerce, culture, and nature.

- <strong>Key Attractions</strong>: 33F free observatory, teamLab Borderless, Azabudai Hills Market
- <strong>Architects</strong>: Main tower by Pelli Clarke & Partners, lower levels by Heatherwick Studio (who designed The Hyundai Seoul)‚Äîthe building itself is a massive work of art.
