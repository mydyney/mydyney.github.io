<h2>ğŸšŒ Access: Direct Bus from Busta Shinjuku</h2>

The most convenient way to get to Kisarazu Outlet is taking the **Direct Express Bus** from **Busta Shinjuku** (Shinjuku Expressway Bus Terminal). It takes about **60 minutes** without transfers.

### 1. Identifying the Bus Terminal
Go to **Busta Shinjuku** connected to the New South Gate of Shinjuku Station. The ticket counters and boarding gates are on the **4th Floor**.

<figure>
  <img src="/images/posts/mitsui-outlet-kisarazu-guide-02.jpg" alt="Busta Shinjuku Entrance">
  <figcaption style="font-size: 0.7em; text-align: center;">Busta Shinjuku (4F)</figcaption>
</figure>

### 2. Purchasing Tickets
You can purchase tickets at the kiosk or counter on the 4th floor.
- **Route:** Shinjuku -> Mitsui Outlet Park Kisarazu
- **Fare:** Approx. 1,600 - 1,700 JPY (One way) / IC Cards accepted
- **Time:** Buses run frequently, but weekends can be busy.

<h2>ğŸ« Must-Do: Get the Coupon Book!</h2>

Upon arrival, **DO NOT** verify brands first. Go straight to the **Information Center** (Tourist Information).
Show your **Passport** (Short-term stay) to receive a **Special Coupon Book** for foreign tourists.

- **Benefits:** Additional 5~10% discount on many brands
- **Condition:** Must present passport (QR code scan available sometimes)
