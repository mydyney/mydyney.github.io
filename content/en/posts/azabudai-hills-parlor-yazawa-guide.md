## Azabudai Hills Travel Q&A

<p><strong>Q. Can I bring kids?</strong></p>
<p><strong>A. Yes!</strong> Despite the name "Adult's Kids Lunch," the menu is family-friendly. Just note that seating is limited, so try to avoid peak hours.</p>

<p><strong>Q. Can I buy a bento without a reservation?</strong></p>
<p><strong>A. Yes.</strong> Everything is made to order, so it may take a little time, but that means you get a freshly prepared, warm bento.</p>

<p><strong>Q. Is there a break time?</strong></p>
<p><strong>A. Yes!</strong> Dine-in service has a <strong>break from 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM</strong>, so plan accordingly. (However, takeout operates without a break!)</p>
