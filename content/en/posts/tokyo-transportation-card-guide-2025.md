### üì± Super Easy! How to Get iPhone Suica/PASMO

*(‚Äª No need to download separate 'Suica' or 'PASMO' apps‚Äîissuing via iPhone's built-in 'Wallet' app is fastest.)*

1. Open iPhone **'Wallet'** app.
2. Tap **'+'** button in top right and select **'Transit Card'**.
3. Choose **'Suica'** or **'PASMO'** from the Japan list.
4. Select charge amount (from 1,000 yen).
5. Pay with Apple Pay's **'Hyundai Card'** and issuance completes instantly!

**‚úÖ Charging Method 1. Apple Pay Charging (Hyundai Card Required)**
Currently, **'Hyundai Card'** is the only card that can be registered with Apple Pay in Korea. Therefore, to issue and charge mobile Suica/PASMO with credit card, you need **Hyundai Card registered with Apple Pay**.

**‚úÖ Charging Method 2. Cash Charging (Solution When You Don't Have Hyundai Card)**
If you don't have a Hyundai Card, you can add Suica/PASMO to your Wallet app and **charge with cash at JR stations, subway stations, or airport ticket machines**! (Thanks to HuchuUnni for this tipüôè)
