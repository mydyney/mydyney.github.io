## How teamLab Borderless Differs from teamLab Planets

<p>teamLab Borderless and teamLab Planets Tokyo are both immersive digital art experiences by teamLab, but they differ significantly in concept and style.</p>

<p>teamLab Borderless removes all boundaries between artworks â€” the entire space is designed like a maze where visitors freely explore and become part of the art. In contrast, teamLab Planets focuses on sensory immersion: visitors take off their shoes and walk through water, physically interacting with the artworks along a set route.</p>

<p>At Borderless, you wander freely through a maze-like space with no fixed path. At Planets, you follow a guided sequence of rooms.</p>

<p>teamLab Planets Tokyo is located at Shintoyosu Station (Yurikamome Line).</p>
