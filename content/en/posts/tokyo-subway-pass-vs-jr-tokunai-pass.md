<h2 id="faq">Frequently Asked Questions (Q&A)</h2>

<strong>Q1: I have a Suica/Pasmo card. Do I need to buy a pass?</strong>

A1: If you're using public transport 2-3 times or less per day, Suica/Pasmo is better. But if you're shopping and sightseeing at multiple locations, a pass is much cheaper.


<strong>Q2: Can I use Tokyo Subway Ticket on JR Yamanote Line?</strong>

A2: Absolutely not. This is the most common mistake! JR gates and subway gates are different, so be careful. Conversely, you cannot use Tokunai Pass on subways either.


<strong>Q3: Is the 72-hour pass the same as a 3-day pass?</strong>

A3: No. It's <strong>hour-based</strong>, not day-based, which is much more advantageous. For example, if you start Friday at 7 PM, you can use it until Monday at 7 PM, making it usable for a 3-night 4-day trip.
