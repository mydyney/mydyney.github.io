### â‘¤ ğŸ§¸ Cat Street & Adults' Paradise 'Kiddyland'

<strong>A leisurely and stylish walking path connecting Harajuku and Shibuya, Cat Street!</strong> At its starting point is 'Kiddyland', the adults' toy store.

- <strong>Kiddyland (KIDDY LAND):</strong> Located on Omotesando Avenue on the way to Cat Street, this place is unmissable. It's a paradise with almost every character goods from Snoopy, Sanrio, Ghibli, Pokemon, and more.
- <strong>Cat Street:</strong> After browsing Kiddyland, walk toward Shibuya to find the quiet and sophisticated Cat Street. Great for strolling with its stylish cafes, local brands, and vintage shops.
