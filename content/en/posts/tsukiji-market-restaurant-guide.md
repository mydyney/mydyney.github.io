### ğŸ•’ Operating Hours: "Morning" is Prime Time!

<p>Tsukiji Market has different atmospheres depending on the time of day.</p>

<ul>
  <li><b>6:00 AM - 9:00 AM:</b> When professionals (chefs, etc.) purchase fresh ingredients. Very crowded and livelyâ€”you'll see the market's true character, but be careful not to obstruct traffic.</li>
  <li><b>9:00 AM - 2:00 PM:</b> Best time for tourists. Most restaurants and street food stalls are actively operating, making it the most comfortable time to enjoy the market.</li>
  <li><b>After 2:00 PM:</b> Many shops start closing, so it's recommended to arrive by 1:00 PM at the latest.</li>
</ul>

### ğŸ—“ï¸ Closed Days: Avoid "Wednesdays" and "Sundays"!

<p>Tsukiji Outer Market is basically closed on <b>Sundays</b> and <b>public holidays</b>. Additionally, many shops close on <b>Wednesdays</b> following the wholesale market's closure schedule.</p>

<p><b>âš ï¸ Important:</b> These closure days are the market's official standards, but some shops in the outer market may close on different days or operate temporarily. <b>If you have a specific shop you want to visit, it's safest to check its operating status on Google Maps before your visit.</b></p>
