## <b>2. Never Get Lost: How to Get There (Yaesu Underground Central)</b>

‚Äã

When you arrive at Tokyo Station, there are dozens of exits and it's confusing, right? Just remember one thing.

<b>"Yaesu Underground Central Exit"</b>

‚Äã

<ol>
<li>After getting off the train, follow the yellow signs toward <b>'Yaesu'</b>.</li>
<li>Find and exit through the <b>Yaesu Underground Central ticket gate</b>.</li>
<li>Right after exiting the ticket gate, you'll see the colorful <b>'Tokyo Character Street'</b> sign straight ahead. (10-second walk!)</li>
</ol>

  <img src="/images/posts/tokyo-station-character-street-guide-03.jpg" alt="JR Tokyo Station Yaesu Underground Central Exit ticket gate">

‚Äã

<b>üí° Tokyo Mate Tip</b>

If you're on the 1st floor (ground level), use the <b>Yaesu Passage</b> or find <b>Daimaru Department Store</b>. Use the stairs going down to the basement right next to the department store to connect directly to Character Street.

## <b>10. Tokyo Station Character Street Tax-Free Guide (1st Floor)</b>

‚Äã

This is the most important information! Tax-free is not available at individual stores. After shopping, you must go to the <b>'1st floor integrated tax-free counter'</b>.

‚Äã

<b>üìç Tax-Free Counter Location</b>

<ul>
<li><b>Location:</b> JR Tokyo Station 1st floor (not basement), next to Sagawa delivery service counter</li>
<li><b>Sign</b>: Red TAX FREE sign</li>
<li><b>Business Hours: 10:00 ~ 21:00</b> (Recommend arriving 30 minutes before closing)</li>
</ul>

‚Äã

<b>ÔøΩ Tax-Free Conditions & Precautions</b>

<ul>
<li><b>Can Combine:</b> Possible if total purchases from multiple stores exceed 5,000 yen excluding tax (5,500 yen including tax) (same day only)</li>
<li>Required Items: Passport (physical), receipts, products, credit card</li>
</ul>

‚Äª Passport, receipt, and credit card names must match.

<ul>
<li>Fee: Refund after deducting approximately 2.1% tax-free fee (actual refund rate around 8% / cash refund not available, refunded to card)</li>
</ul>
