## Sky Room View

<p>As the closest vantage point to Tokyo Tower, it feels like you're looking at it face-to-face from the sky. On a clear day, you can see all the way to Rainbow Bridge.</p>

<figure>
  <img src="/images/posts/azabudai-hills-sky-room-sunset-view-16.jpg" alt="Panoramic view of Tokyo Tower from Sky Room on the 34th floor">
  <figcaption style="font-size: 0.7em; text-align: center;">Tokyo Tower panoramic view from Sky Room</figcaption>
</figure>

<p>The 34th floor has bar-style counter seating, and the staircase connecting to the 33rd floor doubles as seating. The high ceilings create a wonderful sense of openness. Around sunset, visitors gather on the stairs waiting for the sunset and Tokyo Tower's evening illumination.</p>

<figure>
  <img src="/images/posts/azabudai-hills-sky-room-sunset-view-17.jpg" alt="Sky Room staircase seating with Tokyo Tower view">
  <figcaption style="font-size: 0.7em; text-align: center;">Staircase seating at Sky Room</figcaption>
</figure>
