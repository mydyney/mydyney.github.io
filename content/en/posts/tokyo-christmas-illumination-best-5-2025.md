<p>The symbol of Tokyo's illuminations. These two massive shopping complexes, separated by just one street, offer distinctly different light festivals.</p>

<div class="info-box">
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Illumination Period:</strong> Nov 13 (Thu) 2025 - Feb 15 (Sun) 2026</li>
    <li><strong>‚Äª Continues after Christmas!</strong></li>
    <li><strong>Lighting Hours:</strong> 16:00-23:00 (Extended to 24:00 in December)</li>
    <li><strong>HOLIDAY 2025 "Disney JOYFUL MOMENTS":</strong> Nov 13 (Thu) 2025 - Jan 4 (Thu) 2026</li>
    <li><strong>Access:</strong> Tokyo Station, Yurakucho Station (5 min walk), Otemachi Station (5 min walk)</li>
    <li><strong>Tip:</strong> Get free views of both illuminations and Tokyo Station night views from the Shin-Marunouchi rooftop terrace!</li>

<h3>4. Shibuya Blue Cave Illumination</h3>

<p>Step into a fantastic blue world. The path from Shibuya Koen Street to Yoyogi Park is completely filled with blue LEDs, creating a mystical experience as if you've entered a cave. This spot draws massive crowds every year as an Instagram-worthy photo location.</p>

<div class="info-box">
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Expected Period:</strong> Dec 4 (Thu) - Dec 25 (Thu) 2025</li>
    <li><strong>Lighting Hours:</strong> Daily 17:00-22:00</li>
    <li><strong>Access:</strong> Shibuya Station, Harajuku Station</li>
    <li><strong>Tip:</strong> Take your time walking through while holding a warm drink, immersing yourself in the blue lights.</li>
  </ul>
